From Wembley to the EFL — this club runs on its supporters. Trust membership funds the coaches, the charity work and the fan voice at the Crown Oil Arena.

✓Discounts with Friends of the Trust — pubs, local businesses and matchday offers
✓Priority booking on Trust coach travel to every away fixture
✓Members' handbook, competitions and the World Cup prediction league
✓A democratic voice — the Trust represents fans to the club and the FSA
