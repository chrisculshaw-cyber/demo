Your Trust this season

Rochdale DS All Stars

Our Trust charity — a local mum-driven group for families affected by Down's syndrome. We funded safe indoor winter training, took the whole All Stars family (mums, dads and siblings) to a Christmas pantomime with our sister organisation Dale 1907, and we're now working towards a significant contribution to speech therapy provision.

Grassroots women's football

A substantial donation to the RAFC Under-12s women's team provided heavy-duty rain wear — keeping souls warm and embracing their enthusiasm. We've also helped drive the reset of the Her Game Too initiative, with HGT ambassadors now firmly part of the Dale family.

Your voice at the club

Our most important work: representing every member — adult, exile and junior — through our CAB representatives, the Trust Club Director, and directly with departments across the football club, all season long.
